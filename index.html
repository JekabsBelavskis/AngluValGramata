The main characters of the book are Dr. Watson and Sherlock Holm, who solve
another mystical event. In this story, a girl named Mary Marston goes to them after
assistance. She talks about the adventurous events in her life.
Mary's father is Captain Arthur Marston, who once worked in the Andaman Islands. Ten years
ago, she received a letter learning of her father's imminent return to England. The badge even showed
the name of the hotel where Arthur Marston has decided to stay. The
daughter was happy about it and looked forward to the day. On that date, she arrived at the hotel indicated in the
letter, but unfortunately did not find her father anywhere. A desperate search began, as a
result of which Arthur Marston was declared missing. Four years later, Mary Marston received an unexpected
gift on her birthday - a very precious pearl from India. A new investigation began
because no one knew who the sender was.
